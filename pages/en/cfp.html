Share an experience!

This year, the call for proposals concerns all interventions at Sud-Web 2018.

There will still be a form of curation, but all subjects will go through this form so that the team can choose the programming in a concerted way.

All forms of talks are possible:

  • talk with visual support(s);
  • lighting talk ;
  • stand-up ;
  • group activities…

We do not want you to feel trapped in the elaboration of a "traditional" talk. Find a format that make sense for you and with which you feel comfortable!

Do you have an idea? Or several? Do not hesitate to apply. You have until: Feburary 16, 2018 at 11:59 pm (Paris Time) to propose a talk (or several).

We remind you that we'll favour:

  • experience feedbacks ;
  • what makes sense in your approach ;
  • what you learned from this experience, the lesson(s) you acquired ;
  • the changes you'll apply when you get back to work on Monday.

Afraid to jump in? Feel free to contact us, we will mentor you so that you are ready on the D day D!
